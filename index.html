About Troye

Troye Sivan Mellet is a South African-born YouTuber, actor, model, and singer that moved to Perth, Australia when he was two years old. Troye released his debut album in 2015 titled "Blue Neighbourhood", but even before that Troye had a decent following from his YouTube personality.
